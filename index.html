Biography

Jing Li is a senior researcher in NLP center, Tencent AI Lab. She has broad research interests on natural language processing and social computing. Particularly, she works on topic modeling, information extraction, discourse analysis, and NLP for social media. She obtained her PhD degree from the Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management, The Chinese University of Hong Kong in July, 2017. Her PhD supervisor is Professor Kam-Fai Wong. Before going to CUHK, She received her B.S. degree from Department of Machine Intelligence, Peking University in July, 2013.
